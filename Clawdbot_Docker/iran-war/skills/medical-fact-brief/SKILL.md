# Medical Fact Brief

When a medical question is received, research and provide a concise summary of the medical facts using reputable sources.

## Core Directives
1. Use professional and accurate medical terminology.
2. Cite all sources clearly.
3. Prioritize information from reliable health organizations such as the WHO, CDC, or the Korea Disease Control and Prevention Agency (KDCA - 질병관리청).
4. Clearly state that the information is for educational purposes and is not a substitute for professional medical advice.

## Structure
- **Situation Summary**: Brief overview of the medical inquiry.
- **Key Facts**: Bulleted list of verified medical information.
- **Safety Warnings**: Highlight any red flags or emergency symptoms.
- **Sources**: Links or references to the original medical documentation.
